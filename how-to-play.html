instructions

Welcome to the World War II Trivia Quiz! Here's how you can play:

  • Enter your username on the home page and click "Start Quiz".
  • You will have a total of 2 minutes to answer all the questions.
  • Each question will have multiple choice answers. Click on a answer to proceed.
  • Your score will be calculated based on the number of correct answers.
  • Try to answer as many questions correctly within the time limit to get the highest score!
  • Good luck and have fun!
